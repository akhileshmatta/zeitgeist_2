WE-CODE HACKATHON EVENT

About

A hackathon's aim is to generate idea of concepts among participants. Hosts might search for potential prototypes that are good candidates for production or even hire a qualified and promising person for their teams.

Zeitgeist_2K23, along with JNTU KAKINADA, brings forth the Hackathon event,WE-CODE that has garnered massive attention amongst the tech heads. This time, Hackathon is back to emphasize teamwork, learning, and innovation through the generation of novel technologies and prototypes that address significant concerns.

Event Format

  • A real world problem will be given to teams at the time of event along with particular time hours.
