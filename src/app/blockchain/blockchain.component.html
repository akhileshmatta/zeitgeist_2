About

"The blockchain is an incorruptible digital ledger of economic transactions that can be programmed to record not just financial transactions but virtually everything of value." - Don Tapscott, Co-founder of the Blockchain Research Institute.

Blockchain Technology has mainly evolved from a perspective of a decentralized financial system and promoting the cashless economy. The transactions made in Bitcoin or another cryptocurrency are recorded publicly and chronologically. This technology has radically improved the supply chain and other transactions network. Throwing light on the humane arena, "Blockchain is not just a protocol, it's not just a technical system, it's an entirely new way of organizing trust."

Objective

The striving motto of this workshop is to provide a multi-faceted view and learnings of “Blockchain & Cryptocurrencies” for the aspirants. The potent takeaways include -

  • An overall understanding of Blockchain and its vital functionalities
  • The major benefits and real-time applications into limelight.
  • Learnings about decentralized networks and cryptocurrencies.
  • A complete overview of Bitcoin and Bitcoin Mining.

Glimpses Of The Workshop

Session I- Introduction to Cryptocurrency

Session II- Overview of Blockchain

Session III: Bitcoin and Blockchain Data Structures

Session IV- Bitcoin Mining
