PRESENTIT EVENT

About

Unveil your ideas and unfold your innovation through PRESENTIT a Research paper presentation event. It is a golden opportunity to present your research work and innovative ideas with us. You will also get a chance to present research papers in front of esteemed judges who have experience in the field of research. So ZEITGEIST 2023 brings you the event PRESENTIT which provides you a grandstand to share your research and develop effective presentation skills. All you need to do is to select a domain specified below and present a research paper using any trending technology. The number of papers you present will help you in exploring hidden talents and you can notice a positive difference in yourself.

Event Format

  • Event is of 2 stages : Screening and Final.
  • In the first stage you need to submit the Title, Abstract, Keywords, and Authors in a single page in pdf format to the mail address presentitzeitgeist@gmail.com at the time of registration.
  • The pdf mailed at the time of registration is reviewed for the first round and the selected papers are admitted to the second round. No resubmission is entertained.
  • In the second stage you need to present the research paper along with PPT.

Guidelines

  • Participation is individual and is open to students who are interested in presenting research papers related to the domain of Computer science.
  • You can submit your own work or you can extend the work in existing papers provided they are currently relevant.
  • The font type of the document should be in Times New Roman and the font size must be 10.
  • The research paper is to be submitted as a hard copy in the second stage.
  • The duration of the PPT presentation should be within 5 to 10 minutes.
