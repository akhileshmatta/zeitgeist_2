About Us

Zeitgeist is a National level Technical Carnival organised by the department of Computer science and engineering, JNTUK on March 25&26. In a nutshell, it brings into limelight the emerging technologies in the present day world and provides an exposure to learn, explore and further ace the domains of choice in CSE. This is an amalgamation of testing both the theoretical and practical approaches towards the subject through various events and workshops on one end and refreshing activities to break through the monotony of everyday lives on the other.

This fest is centred in the town of Kakinada at UCEK, JNTU. We, the Computer Science students are designing the entire arena in a manner that gives guidance and hands-on exposure on cutting-edge technologies like Blockchain, Data science at the potent best. The broad spectrum of events to be held also range from inquisitive quiz contests to innovative project presentations. Complementing these technical aspects, the cultural fest, Cultivo and fun-filled activities are going to help participants break the ice and experience delight in the entire aura.

On this exuberant note, we wholeheartedly welcome the students to come and join us in Zeitgeist 2023.
