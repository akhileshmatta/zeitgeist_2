Project Presentation

About

We bring you “Project Presentations” to enhance your ability to deliver PowerPoint presentations and to improve your technical skills as an individual. The presentation complements the project demo. It gives evaluators a chance to clear up doubts by asking questions on the spot. These presentations help to understand the main motive of the project.

Some evaluators prefer to attend the presentation first and read the report later. In such a case, the presentation creates the first impression of your project in the mind of the evaluator. It's mandatory to show the implementation of your project. In case of no implementation of your project, the team will be disqualified in the second round.

Event Format

  • Event is of 2 stages
  • Round 1: Every team is allowed to submit the abstract of their project. Among them, 10 - 15 teams were selected and they will send for round 2.
  • Round 2: The teams will show their project presentation and the implementation of their project and the final winners will be announced before march 26th.
